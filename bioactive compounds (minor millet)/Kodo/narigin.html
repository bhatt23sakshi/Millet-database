Naringin

Naringin is a flavanone glycoside and a source of bioactive compound in citrus fruits [1] . It possesses anti-apoptotic, anticarcinogenic, anti-inflammatory, antiulcer, anti-osteoporotic, and antioxidant activity [2] . Naringin increases the alkaline phosphatase (ALP) activity [3] , osteocalcin [4] , and osteocalcin activity [5] . Khare et al. reported 11.97 ppm quantity of naringin in kodo millets with various health benefits [6].

Structure of Naringin

IUPAC name

7-[[2-O-(6-deoxy-α-L-mannopyranosyl)- β-D-glucopyranosyl]oxy]-2,3-dihydro-5- hydroxy-2-(4-hydroxyphenyl)-4H-1- benzopyran-4-one

Chemical formula

C27H32O14

Health benefits

  • Anti-apoptotic
  • Anticarcinogenic
  • Antiinflammatory
  • Antiulcer
  • Antiosteoporotic
